MIT App Inventor: Paint Pot

September 2017 to October 2017, Computer Science//Software

The objective of this project was to get familiar with App Inventor and how it works. My partner and I took on the role of coder. We both had made an app with similar ideas. These ideas became part of the final version of the game. Through the project, I learned how to make a share feature in an app. I also learned how to save a drawing to the device’s storage. My partner and I encountered a few challenges of our own. A challenge was figuring out the layout of the features. We both had very different locations for some of the features in the app. In the end, we compromised a place for the features to go. My partner and I got along well and got a good score on this project as well.
